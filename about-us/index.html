About Us

Simplifying Your K-Electric Bill Experience

Who We Are

KE Duplicate Bill is a free, user-friendly online platform designed to help the citizens of Karachi easily access their electricity bills. We understand the hassle of lost bills or delayed delivery, and our mission is to provide a seamless digital solution for checking, viewing, and downloading K-Electric duplicate bills instantly.

Our Mission

Our goal is to empower consumers with information. Beyond just bill checking, we provide comprehensive guides on:

  • Understanding Unit Prices and Tariff Slabs
  • Load Shedding Schedules
  • New Connection Procedures
  • Tax Certificates and Refunds
