📋 How to Check Your KE Bill

1

Find Your Account Number

Locate your 13-digit K-Electric account number on any previous bill. It's usually at the top of the bill.

2

Enter Account Number

Type your 13-digit account number in the bill checker above and click "Submit" or "Check Bill".

3

View & Download

Your bill details will appear instantly. You can view the amount due, due date, and download a PDF copy.

4

Pay Your Bill

Use our payment methods guide to pay your bill online or offline.
