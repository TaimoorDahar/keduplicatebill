Editorial Guidelines

Our Standard for Accurate, Helpful K-Electric Information

At KE Duplicate Bill, we are committed to providing accurate, up-to-date, and helpful information about K-Electric bills and services to consumers in Karachi. This page outlines our editorial process and the standards we hold ourselves to.

Our Content Mission

Our primary goal is to empower K-Electric consumers with clear, actionable information so they can:

  • Easily access and download their electricity bills
  • Understand their bill charges, tariffs, and slabs
  • Navigate KE services such as new connections, refunds, and net metering
  • Stay informed about load shedding schedules and outage updates

Research & Accuracy Standards

  • Primary Sources: All tariff rates, policy information, and service procedures are sourced directly from K-Electric's official website and NEPRA notifications.
  • Regular Updates: Content is reviewed and updated whenever K-Electric announces changes to tariffs, policies, or services.
  • Fact-Checking: All factual claims are verified against official KE announcements before publication.
  • Transparency: We clearly state when information was last updated on each page.

Content Creation Process

  1. Topic Research: We identify topics based on common consumer questions and search trends.
  2. Source Verification: Information is gathered from official K-Electric sources, NEPRA, and government notifications.
  3. Writing: Content is written in clear, simple language accessible to all consumers.
  4. Review: All content is reviewed for accuracy and completeness before publication.
  5. Publication: Content is published with a clear date and author attribution.
  6. Maintenance: Pages are periodically reviewed and updated to reflect current information.

Independence & Transparency

Independence: KE Duplicate Bill is an independent informational website. We are not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to K-Electric (KE) in any way. Our content is produced independently.

Advertising: Our website displays Google AdSense advertisements. Ads do not influence our editorial content or recommendations.

Accuracy Disclaimer: While we strive for accuracy, tariff rates and service policies change frequently. Always verify critical information directly with K-Electric at 118 or ke.com.pk.

Corrections Policy

If you identify inaccurate or outdated information on our website, please contact us. We take corrections seriously and will review and update content promptly. We clearly acknowledge significant corrections.
