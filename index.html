How to View and Download Your KE Duplicate Bill Online

Lost your paper bill or need another copy? K-Electric (KE) makes it easy to get a duplicate bill anytime — online, on WhatsApp, or by SMS. This guide walks you through each method. You can also use the Electricity Bill Calculator to estimate your next bill before it arrives.

Step-by-Step: View and Download Your KE Bill Online

It takes less than a minute. Here's what to do:

  1. Visit the Official Website: Go to keduplicatebill.com.pk.
  2. Enter Your 13-Digit Account Number: Locate your 13-digit account number on your previous bill. Enter it in the provided field above.

Note: If you're unsure where to find your account number, check the top section of your bill under "Consumer Details."

Get Your KE Bill on WhatsApp (Quickest Method)

K-Electric's WhatsApp chatbot at 0348-0000118 is the fastest way to get your duplicate bill. No website, no login — just a quick chat on your phone.

How to Start

  • Save 0348-0000118 in your contacts.
  • Open WhatsApp and send "Hi" to that number.
  • Select "Duplicate Bill" from the menu and enter your account number.
💬 Start WhatsApp Chat

Services Available via WhatsApp

  • Duplicate Bill Download – PDF or image copy instantly
  • Bill Status Check – View charges or confirm recent payments
  • Power Status – Check outages or report technical issues
  • Load Shedding Schedule – Area-specific outage timings
  • Billing Complaints – File billing or meter reading issues
  • Tax Certificate – Request income tax certificate
  • New Connection Guidance – Apply for a new meter

Standard data charges from your mobile provider may apply.

Check Your KE Bill via SMS

No internet? Use K-Electric's SMS service to check your bill amount and due date. Send to 8119 from any mobile number:

  1. Register: Type REG [13-digit A/C#] → send to 8119
  2. Check Bill: Type BILL [13-digit A/C#] → send to 8119

Additional SMS Commands

  • Technical Complaint: COMP [A/C#] → 8119
  • Load Shed Schedule: LS [A/C#] → 8119
  • Unsubscribe: UNREG [A/C#] → 8119

How to Reduce Your KE Bill

If your KE bill is consistently high, there are a few practical options. Shifting heavy appliances (ACs, geysers) to off-peak hours can lower your charges. For a long-term solution, K-Electric Net Billing lets you install solar panels and sell excess electricity back to KE — reducing your monthly bill significantly. How to Pay Your K-Electric Bill

Pay your KE bill conveniently through any of these options:

  • Online Banking: HBL, UBL, MCB, Meezan Bank internet/mobile banking
  • Mobile Apps: Easypaisa, JazzCash, NayaPay, or the KE app
  • Physical Centers: K Electric offices in DHA, Gulistan-e-Johar, North Nazimabad, and more

After paying, you can verify the status by entering your 13-digit account number on ke.com.pk or by checking via WhatsApp. Payments usually reflect within 24–48 hours. See our full K-Electric payment methods guide for more options.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where can I find my Tariff information on my K Electric bill?
Look under the Consumer Details section of your bill. For full details, refer to the latest tariff notified by the Government of Pakistan at ke.com.pk/tariff-structure.
Is K Electric privately owned?
Yes. K Electric was privatised in 2005. The government now holds only a small stake; the majority is privately owned.
What is the WhatsApp number for K Electric duplicate bill?
Save 0348-0000118 and send "Hi" to start a chat. Select the duplicate bill option to receive your bill instantly.
Where is my K Electric account number on the bill?
Your 13-digit account number is printed prominently at the top of your bill, near your customer information section.
Who is the CEO of K-Electric?
The current CEO is Syed Moonis Abdullah Alvi, who has over 30 years of finance experience and has led K-Electric since 2018.
How do I apply for Net Billing (solar)?
Email net.metering@ke.com.pk or visit the Net Billing page for the full application guide and list of approved vendors.
What are peak electricity hours in Karachi?
April–October: 6:30 PM – 10:30 PM • November–March: 6:00 PM – 10:00 PM. Rates are higher during these windows.
Where is K-Electric's head office?
KE House, 39-B, Sunset Boulevard, Phase-II, Defence Housing Authority, Karachi. Customer care: 118 or 99000.
How do I check my electricity bill by SMS?
Type BILL [13-digit A/C#] and send to 8119. You will receive your bill amount, due date, and status by return SMS.
How do I register a complaint with K-Electric?
Call 118, use WhatsApp (0348-0000118), or visit the Complaint Registration page for the online form.
What is the difference between account number and consumer number on a KE bill?
Your account number is 13 digits — use this for bill checks, SMS service, and online tools. Your consumer number is 8 digits — it identifies your specific connection point and is used for complaints or technical requests.
How long does a KE online payment take to show on the bill?
Most online payments (JazzCash, Easypaisa, bank apps) reflect within 24–48 hours. If your payment doesn't appear after 48 hours, call KE at 118 with your transaction ID. You can also check via WhatsApp.
What happens if I miss the KE bill due date?
A Late Payment Surcharge (LPS) applies — 5% if you pay within 3 days after the due date, and 10% after that. Pay as soon as possible to avoid the higher surcharge.
