How to View and Download Your KE Duplicate Bill Online – A Complete Guide

K-Electric (KE) is Karachi's primary electricity provider, ensuring reliable power supply to homes, businesses, and industries. Whether you've misplaced your bill or need a copy for record-keeping, obtaining a duplicate electricity bill has never been easier. This guide will walk you through downloading your KE duplicate bill online, via WhatsApp, SMS, and more. You can also use our Electricity Bill Calculator to estimate your monthly costs. Let's get started!

How to View and Download Your KE Duplicate Bill Online

Accessing your KE duplicate bill online is quick and straightforward. Follow these steps:

  1. Visit the Official Website: Go to keduplicatebill.com.pk.
  2. Enter Your 13-Digit Account Number: Locate your 13-digit account number on your bill (highlighted in red in the image above). Enter it in the provided field.

📌 Note

If you're unsure where to find your account number, check the top section of your bill under "Consumer Details."

KE Duplicate Bill via SMS

K-Electric’s SMS service lets you check your KE bill online or register for updates using your KE bill account number. Here’s how:

  1. Register for SMS Service: Type REG [13-digit A/C#] (e.g., REG 04000***) and send to 8119. You’ll receive a confirmation for the KE bill registration.
  2. Check Your Bill: Type BILL [13-digit A/C#] and send to 8119. Receive your KE bill details, including amount, KE bill due date, and KE bill status.

Additional SMS Services

  • Technical Complaints: Type COMP [13-digit A/C#] and send to 8119.
  • Load-Shed Schedule: Type LS [13-digit A/C#] and send to 8119.
  • Unsubscribe: Type UNREG [13-digit A/C#] and send to 8119 for KE bill unsubscribe.

K Electric SMS Unsubscription Instructions

To unsubscribe from the SMS service, follow these steps:

  • Type UNREG (space) followed by your 13-digit A/C#.
  • Send the message to 8119.
