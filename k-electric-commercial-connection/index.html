🏢 Commercial & Industrial Connections

Powering Your Business with K-Electric

Starting a new business in Karachi? Reliable electricity is the backbone of any commercial enterprise. This guide covers everything you need to know about K-Electric Commercial Connections, including current tariff rates, application procedures, and industrial load management.

📊 Commercial Tariff Rates 2025

Commercial tariffs are generally higher than residential rates. Here is the current breakdown:

Category Load (kW) Rate/Unit (Rs.)
A2-1 (General) Less than 5 kW 38.50
A2-2 (General) 5 kW and above 42.00
A2-3 (Peak) Time of Use 48.50
A2-3 (Off-Peak) Time of Use 36.00

*Rates are subject to FCA and government taxes.

🏭 Industrial Connections (B-Tariff)

For factories and large-scale industries, K-Electric offers specialized Industrial Tariffs (B1, B2, B3).

  • B1: Up to 25 kW load
  • B2: 26 - 500 kW load
  • B3: For all loads at 11 kV

Industrial consumers often benefit from lower base rates but have higher fixed charges and strict power factor penalties.

📝 How to Apply for a Commercial Connection

  1. Online Application: Visit the KE New Connection portal.
  2. Select Category: Choose "Commercial" or "Industrial".
  3. Documents Required:
    • CNIC of Owner/Director
    • Proof of Ownership/Tenancy Agreement
    • Trade License / NTN Certificate
    • Company Letterhead request
  4. Site Survey: KE team visits to assess load and infrastructure.
  5. Demand Note: Pay the connection charges.
  6. Installation: Meter is installed within 15-20 days.

💡 Tips for Businesses

Reduce Your Commercial Bill

Power Factor Correction: Install capacitors to maintain a power factor above 0.9 to avoid penalties.

Peak Hour Management: Shift heavy machinery usage to off-peak hours (after 10 PM) to save significantly on rates.

❓ FAQs

What is the cost of a commercial meter?

The cost varies based on load. A basic 5kW commercial connection starts from approximately Rs. 35,000 - 50,000.

Can I convert my residential meter to commercial?

Yes, if you are running a business from home, you must apply for a Change of Tariff (COT) to avoid penalties for misuse of tariff.
