K-Electric Commercial Connection Guide

Everything your business needs to know about applying, tariffs, and managing a KE connection.

Getting a K-Electric commercial connection involves a few steps — online application, a site survey, and a demand note payment. This guide walks you through the current tariff rates, required documents, and the full process so you know what to expect before you begin.

Commercial Tariff Rates 2026

Commercial tariffs are generally higher than residential rates. Here is the current breakdown:

Category Load (kW) Rate/Unit (Rs.)
A2-1 (General) Less than 5 kW 38.50
A2-2 (General) 5 kW and above 42.00
A2-3 (Peak) Time of Use 48.50
A2-3 (Off-Peak) Time of Use 36.00

*Rates are subject to FCA and government taxes.

Industrial Connections (B-Tariff)

For factories and large-scale industries, K-Electric offers specialized Industrial Tariffs (B1, B2, B3).

  • B1: Up to 25 kW load
  • B2: 26 - 500 kW load
  • B3: For all loads at 11 kV

Industrial consumers often benefit from lower base rates but have higher fixed charges and strict power factor penalties.

How to Apply for a Commercial Connection

  1. Online Application: Visit the KE New Connection portal.
  2. Select Category: Choose "Commercial" or "Industrial".
  3. Documents Required:
    • CNIC of Owner/Director
    • Proof of Ownership/Tenancy Agreement
    • Trade License / NTN Certificate
    • Company Letterhead request
  4. Site Survey: KE team visits to assess load and infrastructure.
  5. Demand Note: Pay the connection charges.
  6. Installation: Meter is installed within 15-20 days.

Tips for Businesses

Reduce Your Commercial Bill

Power Factor Correction: Install capacitors to maintain a power factor above 0.9 to avoid penalties.

Peak Hour Management: Shift heavy machinery usage to off-peak hours (after 10 PM) to save significantly on rates.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the cost of a commercial meter in Karachi?
The cost varies based on load. A basic 5 kW commercial connection starts from approximately Rs. 35,000 to Rs. 50,000, covering the meter, service connection, and security deposit.
Can I convert my residential meter to commercial?
Yes, if you are running a business from home, you must apply for a Change of Tariff (COT) to avoid penalties for misuse of tariff.
What documents do I need for a K-Electric commercial connection?
You need: CNIC of the owner or director, proof of ownership or tenancy agreement, trade license or NTN certificate, and a company letterhead request letter.
How long does a commercial connection take after applying?
After a site survey and demand note payment, K-Electric typically installs the meter within 15 to 20 working days.
What is the difference between A2 and B tariff in K-Electric?
A2 tariff is for general commercial consumers such as shops and offices. B tariff is for industrial consumers like factories. B1 covers loads up to 25 kW, B2 covers 26 to 500 kW, and B3 is for large industries connected at 11 kV.
What is a power factor penalty and how do I avoid it?
K-Electric applies a power factor penalty if your power factor drops below 0.9. Installing power factor correction capacitors keeps it above that threshold and removes the penalty charge from your bill.
