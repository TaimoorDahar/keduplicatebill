How to Register a K-Electric Complaint

File your complaint online, by SMS, WhatsApp, or helpline and get a tracking ID.

You can file a K-Electric complaint in minutes using the KE Live App, website, SMS, or by calling 118. Once registered, you get a reference number so you can track progress and follow up if needed.

1. Register Complaint via KE Live App

The KE Live App is the fastest way to lodge complaints. Available for both Android and iOS.

  1. Download the KE Live app.
  2. Log in with your account details.
  3. Tap on the "Complaint" section.
  4. Select the type of issue (Power, Billing, etc.).
  5. Submit your request and get a tracking ID.

2. Online Complaint Portal

You can also register complaints directly through the K-Electric website:

  • Visit the KE Complaint Page.
  • Fill in your Account Number and contact details.
  • Describe your issue in the provided form.
  • Submit to receive a reference number.

3. Complaint via SMS

For quick registration of technical faults or outages:

Type COMP [space] Your 13-digit Account Number and send it to 8119.

Example: COMP 0400012345678

4. KE Helpline Numbers

Call 24/7 customer support for immediate assistance:

  • 118 (General Helpline)
  • 99000 (Customer Care)
  • 021-32637133 (Head Office)

Escalating Your Complaint (NEPRA)

Still not resolved?

If K-Electric fails to resolve your issue within a reasonable time, you can escalate the matter to the National Electric Power Regulatory Authority (NEPRA).

NEPRA Consumer Affairs Division:

  • Email: cad@nepra.org.pk
  • Phone: 051-2013200
  • Online Form: nepra.org.pk

Frequently Asked Questions

How can I track my KE complaint status?
You can track your complaint via the KE Live App or by sending STATUS [space] Account Number to 8119.
What is the email address for K-Electric complaints?
Email customer.care@ke.com.pk for general complaints. To report electricity theft, use speakup@ke.com.pk.
Can I file a complaint on WhatsApp?
Yes. Save 0348-0000118 and send "Hi" to start the WhatsApp bot. Select the complaint option from the menu to lodge your issue.
How long does KE take to resolve a complaint?
Technical faults like outages are usually attended to within hours. Billing disputes may take a few working days. If unresolved, escalate to NEPRA.
What if K-Electric does not resolve my complaint?
You can escalate to NEPRA's Consumer Affairs Division. Email cad@nepra.org.pk or call 051-2013200. You can also submit a complaint through nepra.org.pk.
