K-Electric New Connection Online Application

Apply online, know the documents you need, and find out what the connection will cost you.

You can apply for a K-Electric new connection entirely online through KE's official portal. The process takes 7 to 15 working days from submission to meter installation, provided all your documents are in order. For business or industrial connections, see our dedicated commercial connection guide.

Step-by-Step Application Process

Step 1: Online Application Form

Visit the official K-Electric new connection portal and fill out the application form with:

  • Personal information (Name, CNIC, Contact)
  • Property details (Address, Area, Ownership type)
  • Connection type: Asaan Meter (below 80 kW), Housing/Multistorey (above 80 kW), or Industrial (above 80 kW)
  • Sanctioned load requirement (in kW)
Apply Online →

Step 2: Document Upload

Required Documents:

Document Details
CNIC Copy Original applicant's CNIC (front & back)
Ownership Proof Sale deed, allotment letter, or registry
NOC (if rented) No Objection Certificate from owner
Witness CNIC Any family member or authorized person
Sanctioned Map Building plan (for new constructions)
Affidavit Signed on Rs. 100 stamp paper

Step 3: Fee Payment

Pay the application processing fee online:

  • Application Fee: Rs. 500 - 2,000 (depends on load)
  • Security Deposit: Rs. 5,000 - 20,000 (refundable)
  • Payment Methods: Bank transfer, JazzCash, Easypaisa

Step 4: Site Survey

K-Electric technical team visits your location within 3-5 working days to:

  • Verify property and documents
  • Assess electrical infrastructure feasibility
  • Determine meter installation point
  • Calculate final connection charges

Step 5: Final Payment & Installation

After survey approval:

  1. Receive demand notice with total charges
  2. Pay connection charges within 30 days
  3. KE installs meter within 7-15 days after payment
  4. Receive confirmation SMS with account number

Common Reasons for Rejection

  • Incomplete Documents: Missing CNIC, ownership proof, or NOC
  • Outstanding Dues: Unpaid bills on existing connections
  • Invalid Ownership: Property documents don't match applicant
  • Illegal Connection History: Previous kunda or theft record
  • Technical Infeasibility: No nearby infrastructure or overloaded area
  • Disputed Property: Legal issues with land ownership

Solution: If rejected, you'll receive specific reasons via SMS. Address the issues and reapply within 30 days.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I apply for a new connection if I am a tenant?
Yes, but you need a No Objection Certificate (NOC) from the property owner along with their CNIC copy and ownership documents.
How long does the entire K-Electric new connection process take?
From application submission to meter installation: 7 to 15 working days if all documents are correct and there are no technical issues.
Can I upgrade my sanctioned load later?
Yes, you can apply for load extension anytime. Additional charges apply based on the increased load capacity. Read our full guide on K-Electric sanctioned load for eligibility and process details.
What if there is no KE infrastructure near my property?
KE will assess the feasibility. If infrastructure development is needed, additional line extension charges may apply and can be Rs. 50,000 or more.
Is the security deposit refundable?
Yes, the security deposit is fully refundable when you permanently disconnect your meter. It is adjusted against the final bill if there is any outstanding amount.
Why was my K-Electric new connection application rejected?
Common reasons include missing documents (CNIC, ownership proof, or NOC), outstanding dues on an existing connection, invalid property documents, or a previous kunda or electricity theft record. You will receive the specific reason via SMS and can reapply within 30 days after fixing the issue.
How do I track my K-Electric new connection application?
After submitting your application, you receive a Case ID and a password via SMS. Log in to the KE tracking portal at new-connections.ke.com.pk and enter these details to check your application status.
