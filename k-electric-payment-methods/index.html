K-Electric Bill Payment Methods 2026

All Ways to Pay Your KE Bill Online & Offline

Need to pay your K-Electric bill? This guide covers all KE bill payment methods including online options (JazzCash, Easypaisa, banking apps), ATMs, and offline payment centers across Karachi.

📱 Mobile Banking (JazzCash & Easypaisa)

JazzCash KE Bill Payment

  1. Open Jazz Cash app or dial *786#
  2. Select "Bill Payment" → "Electricity"
  3. Choose "K-Electric"
  4. Enter 13-digit Consumer Number
  5. Verify bill amount and pay
  6. Receive SMS confirmation instantly

Charges: Rs. 20-50 service fee | Limit: Up to Rs. 100,000

Easypaisa Electricity Bill Payment

  1. Open Easypaisa app or visit retailer
  2. Tap "Bill Payment" → "Electricity"
  3. Select "K-Electric Karachi"
  4. Enter Consumer ID (13 digits)
  5. Confirm amount and complete payment
  6. Save digital receipt

Charges: Rs. 0-30 (depends on amount) | Available: 24/7

🏧 ATM & 1BILL Payment

1Link ATM Payment (No Charges)

Pay at any 1Link ATM across Pakistan:

  1. Insert your ATM card
  2. Select "Bill Payment" option
  3. Choose "Electricity" → "K-Electric"
  4. Enter 13-digit Consumer Number
  5. Verify bill details
  6. Confirm payment
  7. Collect printed receipt

Benefit: Zero service charges | Available: 24/7 at 14,000+ ATMs

1BILL Payment Portal

Use dedicated 1BILL kiosks or online portal:

  • Visit 1bill.com.pk
  • Register or login
  • Add K-Electric as biller
  • Pay via debit/credit card

📲 KE Live App Payment

Official K-Electric Mobile App

The most convenient method with additional features:

How to Pay:

  1. Download KE Live app (Android/iOS)
  2. Register with Consumer Number
  3. View current bill
  4. Tap "Pay Now"
  5. Choose payment method (card/mobile wallet)
  6. Complete secure payment

Additional Features:

  • View payment history
  • Download duplicate bills
  • Set payment reminders
  • Report power outages
  • Track complaints

🏦 Bank Branches & KE Offices

In-Person Payment Options

Bank Branches

Visit any branch of participating banks with your bill:

  • Bring original bill or Consumer Number
  • Fill deposit slip (if required)
  • Pay cash or via account transfer
  • Collect stamped receipt

Timings: Monday-Friday 9 AM - 5 PM | Saturday 9 AM - 1 PM

KE Customer Care Centers

Pay directly at K-Electric offices:

  • Gulshan Office: Block 13-D, Gulshan-e-Iqbal
  • North Nazimabad: Block L, Main Road
  • Malir Office: Malir Cantt Area
  • Payment Methods: Cash, credit/debit cards

Timings: Monday-Saturday 8 AM - 8 PM

💡 Payment Tips & Important Notes

  • Payment Reflection: Online payments reflect within 24-48 hours
  • Due Date: Pay before due date to avoid late payment charges (10% penalty)
  • Save Receipts: Keep all payment receipts for 6 months
  • Verify Amount: Always check bill amount before paying
  • Auto-Pay: Set up standing instructions with your bank to never miss payments
  • Confirmation SMS: You should receive SMS from KE within 48 hours confirming payment

❓ FAQs

Which payment method is fastest?

Mobile banking (JazzCash/Easypaisa) and KE Live app are fastest, with instant confirmation and payment reflection within 24 hours.

Can I pay KE bill from any bank account?

Yes, most banks support K-Electric bill payment through their internet/mobile banking. Check with your bank's app.

What if my payment doesn't reflect on my bill?

Wait 48 hours. If still not reflected, contact KE helpline 118 with your payment receipt/transaction ID.

Are there any charges for online payment?

Most methods charge Rs. 0-50 service fee. ATM payments via 1Link are completely free.
