K Electric Schedule 2025: Maintenance, Load Shedding, and Light Timings

Understanding the K Electric schedule for managing your daily activities is essential. In this article, we will cover K Electric maintenance schedules, new load-shedding schedules, and complaints. Furthermore, we'll tell you about load shedding and light schedules. Stay informed with our guide.

K Electric Schedules

K Electric schedules keep Karachi residents updated on power availability and planned maintenance. These schedules help you plan your day around electricity supply and avoid inconvenience.

Types of K Electric Schedules

  • Load Shedding Schedule: Planned power outages in different areas
  • Maintenance Schedule: Scheduled maintenance work on infrastructure
  • Light Schedule: Power restoration timings after outages

How to Check K Electric Load Shedding Schedule

To check the latest load shedding schedule for your area:

  1. Visit the official K-Electric website: www.ke.com.pk
  2. Call the KE helpline: 118
  3. Send a WhatsApp message to: 0348-0000118
  4. Check KE's official social media pages for updates

📱 Quick Contact Options

  • Helpline: 118 (24/7)
  • WhatsApp: 0348-0000118
  • Website: www.ke.com.pk
  • SMS: Send "SCHEDULE" to 8119

K Electric Maintenance Schedule

K-Electric regularly conducts maintenance work to improve infrastructure and ensure reliable power supply. Maintenance schedules are announced in advance through:

  • SMS notifications to registered customers
  • Official website announcements
  • Social media updates
  • Local area notices

Understanding Load Shedding Areas

K-Electric categorizes areas based on payment recovery and technical losses:

High Recovery Areas

Areas with good payment history experience minimal or no load shedding.

Low Recovery Areas

Areas with poor payment recovery may face scheduled load shedding to manage demand.

Peak Hours and Off-Peak Hours

Understanding peak hours can help you manage electricity usage:

  • Peak Hours (Summer): 7:00 PM to 11:00 PM
  • Peak Hours (Winter): 6:00 PM to 10:00 PM
  • Off-Peak Hours: Late night to early morning

How to File a Complaint

If you experience unscheduled power outages or have concerns about the schedule:

  1. Call KE helpline: 118
  2. Use the KE mobile app
  3. Send a complaint via WhatsApp: 0348-0000118
  4. Visit the nearest KE customer center
  5. File an online complaint at www.ke.com.pk

Tips to Manage During Load Shedding

  • Keep backup power solutions (UPS, generator) ready
  • Charge electronic devices during power availability
  • Plan heavy appliance usage during off-peak hours
  • Keep emergency lights and torches accessible
  • Store water for daily needs

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How can I check my area's load shedding schedule?

A: Call 118, send a WhatsApp message to 0348-0000118, or visit www.ke.com.pk for the latest schedule.

Q: Why is there unscheduled load shedding in my area?

A: Unscheduled outages can occur due to technical faults, overloading, or emergency maintenance. Report it to 118 immediately.

Q: Can I get SMS alerts for load shedding?

A: Yes, register your mobile number with KE to receive SMS notifications about scheduled outages and maintenance.
