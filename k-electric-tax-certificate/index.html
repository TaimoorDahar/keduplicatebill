K-Electric Tax Certificate Download

Get your withholding tax certificate online, on WhatsApp, or from any KE service center.

Your K-Electric tax certificate shows the advance income tax deducted from your electricity bills. You need it when filing your annual tax return with FBR to claim a refund or adjust the amount already paid.

Method 1: Download via KE Website

  1. Visit the official K-Electric Website.
  2. Navigate to the "Customer Services" section.
  3. Click on "Tax Certificate".
  4. Enter your 13-digit Account Number.
  5. Select the Fiscal Year (e.g., 2025-2026).
  6. Click "Generate" to download the PDF certificate.

Method 2: Get via WhatsApp

The easiest way to get your certificate is through the KE WhatsApp bot:

  1. Save the number 0348-0000118 in your contacts.
  2. Send a message saying "Hi".
  3. Select the option for "Tax Certificate" from the menu.
  4. Enter your Account Number when prompted.
  5. Receive your certificate instantly as a PDF file.

Method 3: Visit KE Customer Care

If you are unable to download it online, visit any nearby K-Electric Customer Care Center (IBC). Bring your:

  • Original CNIC
  • Copy of latest electricity bill

The representative will print the certificate for you free of charge.

Why Do You Need This Certificate?

The government charges Advance Income Tax (7.5% or more) on electricity bills exceeding Rs. 25,000 (amount varies by filer status). This amount is adjustable. By submitting this certificate to the FBR while filing your annual returns, you can claim a refund or adjustment for the tax already paid.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a fee for the K-Electric tax certificate?
No, the certificate is issued free of cost whether you get it online, via WhatsApp, or at a KE customer care center.
Can I get a certificate for previous years?
Yes. The online portal and WhatsApp service let you select a previous fiscal year to download historical certificates.
What is the KE WhatsApp number for the tax certificate?
Save 0348-0000118 and send "Hi" to start the chat. Select the Tax Certificate option and enter your account number when asked.
Why do I need a withholding tax certificate from KE?
It shows the advance income tax deducted from your electricity bills. You submit it to FBR when filing your annual tax return to get a refund or adjustment on tax already paid.
What documents do I need at a KE customer care center?
Bring your original CNIC and a copy of your latest electricity bill. The representative will print the certificate for you at no charge.
